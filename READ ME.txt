*At the entrance of the project, play,quit and score parameters are located in the main menu
*In the game, the user earns points by moving(by mouse right Click) a ball on randomly spawned floors.
*The speed of the ball increases over time.
*The floors on which the user passes are deleted after a certain period of time in order not to tire the computer.
*The camera moves relative to the ball.
*At the end of the game, the main menu is redirected again and the points earned by the user are displayed in the text.
 If the player wants to start a new game, the new game is started by clicking the play button again.
